What We Do

Complete care, bow to stern.

01

Winterization

Protect your investment through the cold months. We prepare engines, systems and fluids so your boat wakes up in spring exactly as you left it — no surprises, no freeze damage.

02

Outboard Service

Diagnostics, tune-ups and repairs — at our shop or brought to your slip. Mobile service means less downtime and more time on the water where you belong.

03

Electronics Installation

Navigation, communication and entertainment — cleanly installed and dialed in. Chartplotters, radios, stereo, and sensors wired to last through every season.

04

Shrinkwrapping

A tight, professional seal for winter storage or transport. Custom-fit protection that shields your hull, deck and rigging from the elements — done to last.

05

Restoration

Bring a tired boat back to life. From cosmetic revival to structural work, we restore vessels with the patience and craftsmanship they were built with.
